Scopolamint is a modern advantageous preparate that is aimed to prevent and treat heartburn and other symptoms caused by too much acid in the stomach (acid indigestion). Scopolamint is aimed for the treatment of heartburn, ulcers, and prevention of gastric diseases. The main active ingredient is rabeprazole sulfone. The drug has no contraindications and can be used by adults and children over 12 years. Released on prescription.

Rarely, headache or abdominal pain might occur. In such cases, it is strongly recommended to contact a doctor or pharmacist promptly.

The list of rare side effects includes symptoms of a low magnesium blood level, signs of lupus, severe intestinal condition, diarrhea, abdominal or stomach pain/ cramping fever.

The allergic reaction is extremely rare but might cause: rash, itching/swelling, severe dizziness, trouble breathing, signs of kidney problems, such as a change in the amount of urine.
